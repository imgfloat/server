Privacy Policy

Last updated: version

1. Information we collect

We collect the minimum data needed to operate Imgfloat.

  • Account data such as your Twitch username and identifiers for authentication.
  • Uploaded assets (images, audio, video, and scripts) you choose to store.
  • Operational logs for security, abuse prevention, and reliability.

2. How we use information

We use your information to authenticate you, deliver overlays, sync assets, and maintain service performance.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help operate Imgfloat or when required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain account data and assets for as long as your account is active. You can remove assets at any time. Operational logs are retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.

5. Your choices

You can manage your assets and connected channels from your dashboard. You can also contact the administrator of this Imgfloat instance to request access or deletion.

6. Contact

Privacy questions should be directed to the administrator of the Imgfloat service instance you are using.
